# Reaction Turbine from Stator
The center **Stator** is perfect for a cheap **Reaction** Turbine! Cross between a Francis & Kaplan Turbine rotor. The water enters actually and accelerates radially spraying out in a disc, transferring momentum to the shaft to spin it in the opposite direction (Newton's third law).

# Impulse Turbine from Impeller 
The other parts **Impeller** can make a very good **Impulse Turbine**! similar to 1/2 of a pelton wheel but the spoons are on the inside instead, as turbine veins. The water comes in at the periphery from nozzles and exits from the center after changing direction.

Impeller can be modified to make a more optimal water exit path by drilling holes near the axle.
